New Memory Garden & Reminiscence Pathway Opens at Waverley

Our beautiful new memory garden featuring themed areas from different decades is now open. The sensory pathway includes familiar plants, sounds, and textures designed to stimulate positive memories and provide therapeutic benefits for our residents with dementia.

Therapeutic Memory Gardens

The new memory garden has been specifically designed to provide therapeutic benefits for residents with dementia, incorporating familiar elements from different time periods to stimulate positive memories.

Reminiscence Pathway Features

  • Decade-Themed Areas: Gardens representing 1940s, 1950s, and 1960s
  • Sensory Plants: Herbs and flowers that stimulate memory through scent
  • Memory Benches: Seating areas with period-appropriate music
  • Interactive Elements: Touch-friendly plants and memory prompts

Dementia Care Benefits

Our memory garden provides a safe, stimulating environment that can help reduce anxiety, improve mood, and encourage social interaction among residents.
