Welcome to Waverley Care Centre

Specialized dementia and memory care with a warm, family-centered approach. We believe in preserving dignity, maintaining connections, and creating meaningful moments in a safe, nurturing environment where every resident is valued as an individual.

At Waverley Care Centre, we understand that dementia affects not just individuals but entire families. Our approach focuses on person-centered care that honors each resident's life story, preferences, and unique needs.

We create a home-like environment where residents feel secure and families feel welcomed. Our specialized dementia care programs are designed to maintain cognitive function, promote social engagement, and enhance quality of life.

Our dedicated team works closely with families to ensure continuity of care and meaningful connections. We believe that small gestures make the biggest difference - a familiar song, a favorite meal, or a cherished memory shared.

The centre is registered to provide specialized dementia nursing, residential dementia care, respite services, and family support programs, all delivered with compassion and expertise in our purpose-designed facility.
