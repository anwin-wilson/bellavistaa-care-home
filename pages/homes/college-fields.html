Welcome to College Fields Nursing Home

Medical excellence in a tranquil parkland setting. We provide comprehensive healthcare services with state-of-the-art medical facilities, rehabilitation programs, and therapeutic environments designed for optimal recovery and long-term care.

College Fields Nursing Home is situated in beautiful parkland surroundings, providing a peaceful environment that promotes healing and recovery. Our facility combines advanced medical care with the therapeutic benefits of nature.

We specialize in complex medical care, post-hospital rehabilitation, and long-term nursing support. Our multidisciplinary team includes doctors, nurses, physiotherapists, and healthcare specialists working together to provide comprehensive care.

Our modern medical facilities include rehabilitation suites, therapy rooms, and specialized equipment to support recovery and maintain health. We believe that quality medical care should be delivered in a comfortable, home-like environment.

The home is registered to provide complex nursing care, medical rehabilitation, post-operative care, chronic disease management, and specialized therapeutic services, all delivered by our expert healthcare team.
