New Coastal Garden & Beach Access Path Opens at Barry

Our beautiful new seaside garden with direct beach access is now open, featuring therapeutic coastal plants, sea-view seating areas, and accessible pathways for all residents to enjoy the healing benefits of our oceanfront location.

Therapeutic Seaside Gardens

The new coastal garden has been specifically designed to maximize the therapeutic benefits of our seaside location, incorporating native coastal plants and providing stunning sea views.

Beach Access Features

  • Accessible Beach Path: Wheelchair-friendly pathway to the beach
  • Sea View Seating: Multiple seating areas overlooking the ocean
  • Coastal Plant Therapy: Sensory garden with sea-loving plants
  • Beach Activity Area: Safe space for supervised beach activities

Seaside Therapy Benefits

Our coastal location provides natural therapeutic benefits including fresh sea air, calming ocean sounds, and vitamin D from seaside sunshine.
