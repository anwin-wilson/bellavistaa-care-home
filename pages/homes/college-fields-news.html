New Rehabilitation Suite & Advanced Medical Equipment at College Fields

Our state-of-the-art rehabilitation suite is now operational, featuring the latest physiotherapy equipment, hydrotherapy facilities, and advanced monitoring systems to support recovery and improve patient outcomes in our peaceful parkland setting.

Advanced Medical Facilities

The new rehabilitation suite represents a significant investment in medical excellence, providing comprehensive facilities for post-operative care, chronic disease management, and therapeutic rehabilitation.

Rehabilitation Suite Features

  • Hydrotherapy Pool: Heated therapeutic pool for aquatic physiotherapy
  • Advanced Gym Equipment: Specialized rehabilitation and mobility equipment
  • Monitoring Systems: Real-time health tracking and progress monitoring
  • Therapy Rooms: Private spaces for occupational and physiotherapy

Multidisciplinary Approach

Our healthcare team includes doctors, nurses, physiotherapists, and occupational therapists working together to provide comprehensive medical care and rehabilitation services.
