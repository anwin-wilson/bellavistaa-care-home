Bellavista Care App

Stay connected with your loved ones with our comprehensive care companion app

Available for iOS and Android devices

App Features

Video Calling

Connect with residents through secure video calls. Schedule calls and receive notifications when your loved one is available.

Health Updates

Receive real-time health updates, medication schedules, and care notes from our professional staff.

Photo Sharing

View daily photos and activities. Share memories and stay connected with your resident's daily life.

Visit Scheduling

Book visits, check availability, and receive reminders. Manage your visit schedule with ease.

Secure Messaging

Communicate directly with care staff, ask questions, and receive updates through secure messaging.

Push Notifications

Receive instant alerts for medication times, emergency updates, visit reminders, and important care announcements.

Offline Mode

Access essential information like contact details, care plans, and recent updates even without internet connection.

Biometric Login

Secure access with fingerprint or face ID authentication. Quick and safe login to protect sensitive information.

GPS Location

Find the nearest Bellavista care home with GPS navigation, directions, and contact information.

Automated Reports

Generate and download comprehensive care summaries, medication reports, and activity logs automatically.
