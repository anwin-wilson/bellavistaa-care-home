Our Care Services & Resources

Comprehensive care services and resources to support you and your loved ones

Our Commitment to Care

Our staff are dedicated to providing specialist care for people in need of long term Nursing, Dementia, and Palliative care, Learning disability, respite care in a warm and friendly environment. We encourage families to become involved in everything we do from the care planning process.

We support Residents' physical health needs, provide accommodation, support 24-hours care support, nutritious meals and day-to-day help as aspects of care. Our care homes also provide additional care from registered nurses and we provide specialist support for our residents living with dementia and nursing needs.

Personalized Care Plans

We help and support people to settle in to their new home, we ask you and your family to help us document important things about your life. With all this information we can create a care package as individual as you are.

Dementia Care

We focus on providing the best person-centred care for people with dementia and mental health problems. Our focus on this specific area allows us to use the knowledge that comes from our experience of the area, and along with our research and past evidence we are able to provide a tailored and person-centred approach to care.

The small things are just as important like personalising a room or filling a memory box, and dementia-friendly features to support people with visual, hearing and mobility impairments. All through the home we enhanced and carefully designed the environment to be Dementia friendly with features themes for a calmer and stimulating atmosphere.

Long Term Nursing

We provide nursing care for individuals with specialist requirements in many of our care homes. Our nursing care teams are fully qualified and have the specialist expertise required to care for residents' varying medical needs and requirements.

People who come to us may be living with conditions such as Parkinson's Disease, Multiple Sclerosis or Motor Neuron Disease; others may have suffered a injury as a result of an accident.

Palliative Care

At Bellavista our palliative care services are provided with the care, compassion and professionalism that both the resident and their families need during this final stage in everyone's life. Our staff will work with medical staff, hospices and other professionals whenever needed in order to provide a comfortable environment for the resident and to relieve suffering as much as possible.

We provide tailored emotional support and practical guidance for families and friends throughout end of life care.

Respite Care

We understand how difficult it can be for families or friends providing ongoing care. Our respite care services provide a crucial break for carers for two weeks plus in planned circumstances or emergency circumstances. Respite care offers families the chance to go on holiday or simply have some well-earned time off.

We also provide convalescent care, to help people who need to recuperate after a hospital stay and to support them to be well enough to go back home.

EMI Nursing & Residential Care

Our residential care offers 24-hour support for older people who, through increasing frailty find it difficult to live independently at home. We do everything we can to make Residents feel safe and confident, and enjoy a fulfilled and happily balanced life within our homes.
